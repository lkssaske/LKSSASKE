LET'S START

The display over other apps feature has been a challenge to most of the Android and iOS users for the New update tech versions. With this option turned off, most of the people are limited things like screen recording, doing live streams and background playing options. This option is always turned off within the system itself only to make your phone faster but deny you the advanced and important use of some features within different apps and platforms. Reading this Tutorial guide will help you with the step by step Tutorial on how to enable the display over other apps feature not available problem and proceed to the features you want to archive.

STEPS TO FOLLOW

Carefully read and follow each step

STEP 1

The most important and the first thing to do is making sure you are connected to to a WiFi or HOTSPOT. This could include with or without internet connection, this does not affect the process.

STEP 2

Download app ops and shizuku on playstore. For the process you will need two important app and they are all available in the Google playstore, so I recommend you download them there.

STEP 3

Mostly and almost all the new devices always come with the developer features turned off, you will have to turn it on yourself from the phone settings to unlock advanced features. Go to your phone settings, then go to the system settings and trace down the phone build number, tap on the build number 7 times to enable the developer option. Once this is done, open your developer option and scroll down to the wireless debugging and turn it on.

STEP 4

Once you are done opening your phone's developer option , the process begins directly from the shizuku app. Open your shizuku app . You will see options like the pairing and Start options. Those are the main set up options you are going to use. Press the pairing button, this will lead you to advanced page with the developer option key.Press the developer option provided, this will lead you to your default phone settings within the developer option. Form there you can now look for the wireless debugging option. Once you get it press slightly besides the turn on and turn off option [ NOT THE OPTION ITSELF ] . This will lead you to your wireless debugging option with the code preferences to choose (QR or PAIRING CODE ) . Don't go for the QR option instead go for the pairing code option. When you tap on it it will give you a code which must carefully be typed in the pop up filling form on your screen. When you are done copying the code , you will be giving a ( pair successful ) feedback.

STEP 5

When the pairing process is done go back to your shizuku app. Remember we had pairing and Start option. Now we are going for the start option. Press start. When you press start in some case the self coding procedure will begin but in most cases it will say " try to disable and enable the wireless debugging option if it keeps searching" this tab gives you a developer option with it. Press the developer option provided. This will take you to your phone settings developer option. Here look for the wireless debugging option. Turn it off then immediately turn it on. Once you've done that go back to your shizuku and the self coding Will begin taking at most 10 seconds. Once the self coding is done you can now go back to the (app ops ) that we downloaded at the start.

STEP 6

Open the ( app ops ) app . Once you are done with the shizuku set up you can now open the app ops. When you open the app, all the apps available in your phone will be analysed. Once the analysis is done. You can now access the full permissions for all the app you have downloaded and the system apps as well.

STEP 7

When you press on any app you want advanced features on you will get multiple features to enable or disable including the display over other apps option. Just press on it then press the allow option and you are all done. Centered Red Title
