FINANCE TACTICS

The next thing is about your finances and how you manage them.This tactics may apply to both the wealthy and the average teams based on their development. Without wasting much time lets get started. What really affects your finances and how to avoid and fix them for a better finance management.To begin with the biggest expense rate is the players and staff wedges. Overpaying Staff members and players will always land you in trouble. The board alway provide you with the recommended wedges to offer players and staffs with when renewing or signing new recruitment. Always offer the provided amount and go up the offer with minimal added cash and not offer up to the agent's amount. If it's rejected and the provided amount is still way too high, just pull out the deal and get another recruitment, but if Incase you are dealing with key player or staff member with a global rank and very important you can go for the deal for better and maintained performance. So how do we avoid this salary drain issues from the staff and players? The best strategy is to sign in highly rated and recommend free agents with high and average ratings and age. This leads to an advantage of lots of players marketing and revenue. Always check at the overall ratings before considering the players promotions. Once you've done the promotion to academy players. You can go directly to the players profile. From this, you can request the players future potential performance and overall ratings. Remember not all players are destined to be good, if the potential report shows the player is to rate below 80, you can consider replacing or giving loan offers, on the other hand if it shows a rate of up to 95 then that's a very good player to keep. The next tip is searching for the young stars. Searching for young Stars is also a good tactic in getting quality players, so just head over to the players search option.
