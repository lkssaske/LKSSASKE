CHELSEA TACTICS

Managing premier League teams always became challenging and more fun as well in FCM 24.
Managing Chelsea might be challenging with it's current seasonal form. Today's guide will provide you with the best tactics to impact on when in management of Chelsea football club Either as a manager, director or the chairman.
The obvious thing to always begin with is the traditional playing style. Remember we are all racing towards the main title which is the premier League cup, and impacting a weakling tactic Will not win you even the smallest title or cup. The teams playing style should always and recommend to passing. Playing in the world's most difficult football league Will always need an organised form during a match. Incase you are a director or a manager, consider getting a good first team manager with more than 86 overall rating. The first thing to consider as a director is to terminate Mouricio porchetino's contract and get a new fresh recruitment with a passing mentality playing style. Anyway that's just a by the way for directors, but most of us play as manager.
Now let's talk about the field tactics. We always starting from the formation. The first preferred main style of play is 4-3-3, and 3-4-1-2 as your secondary formation tactic.
These formation can also be changed if you have less players who can fit into it. Getting into the field manipulation and playing. Shooting tactic is always considered great to set. Considering the current Chelsea form and available players would go for a balanced shooting tactic. But if improve your squad further and stronger consider using the patient tactic. The squad is not that good but not bad as well, so we go in patient style. Let's move on to the final third of the game. Chelsea is a great team with dozen of fans and supporters. Despite it's form, the players are still able to take the chance and coordinate well. Final third is set to patient making them aquire patience towards the coordination assists and taking chances towards goal scoring. Talking about the game exploit. We already explained this more than enough times. No need to argue or repeat it. Always exploit your opponent from both sides or in a balanced way, or with you advanced side. You know what I mean by that. It's well explained in many universal videos. Let's get to the style of play. Setting this to the same one as the traditional playing style which is the passing style is very important. But this is Chelsea and sometimes breaking rules always help at difficult or complicated situations, you can also do a counter attack strategy just incase. Moving to the in possession form of playing. We all talk about this. The player are already average despite not being good. Getting an attacking set up is the key. Remember the players are already talking their chances in a maximum way up the field and wouldn't hesitate to hold the back. If you do this they might fail to score. Let's get to the defending tactics. Chelsea defence is not that bad, in fact it's just perfect. Without much words, just set your defending tactic to press high defending. Remember this is premier League title. Using a deep defending may also work only Incase of high gaming pressure. All teams in the premier League are aggressive and and any team may forcefully stump down your defense and score. So that's the tutorial guide for managers and directors in the FCM 24 game.
