Here is a step-by-step tutorial on how to fix a black screen issue in the Winning Eleven game:

Step 1: Restart your device

The first thing you should try is to restart your device. Sometimes, a simple reboot can fix various issues, including black screen problems.

Step 2: Update your graphics drivers

Outdated graphics drivers can often cause black screen issues in games. To update your graphics drivers, go to the manufacturer's website (NVIDIA, AMD, or Intel) and download the latest drivers for your specific graphics card model.

Step 3: Check for game updates

Make sure that your Winning Eleven game is up to date. Developers often release patches and updates to fix bugs and improve performance. Check for updates in the game's settings or on the developer's website.

Step 4: Verify game files

If you are playing the game on a platform like Steam, you can verify the game files to ensure that all game files are intact and not corrupted. To do this, right-click on the game in your Steam library, select Properties, go to the Local Files tab, and click on Verify Integrity of Game Files.

Step 5: Check your system requirements

Make sure that your device meets the minimum system requirements to run the game. If your device does not meet the requirements, you may experience performance issues, including black screens.

Step 6: Disable overlays and background applications

Certain overlays and background applications (like Discord, NVIDIA GeForce Experience, or MSI Afterburner) can interfere with your game and cause black screen problems. Try disabling these overlays and applications before launching the game.

Step 7: Adjust game settings

Sometimes, changing the graphics settings in the game can help fix black screen issues. Try lowering the graphics settings (like resolution, texture quality, or shadows) and see if the issue is resolved.

Step 8: Reinstall the game

If none of the above steps work, you may need to reinstall the game. Uninstall the game from your device, download a fresh copy from the developer's website or platform, and reinstall it.

NOTE :
If you have tried all of the above steps and are still experiencing a black screen issue in the Winning Eleven game, you may want to contact the game's developer or technical support for further assistance.
